Seller Responsibilities

As a seller, you agree to:

Platform Fee

SquidBay charges a 10% platform fee on all marketplace transactions (Stripe Connect application_fee_amount). This fee is collected by Stripe at charge time; the remaining 90% routes to the seller's Stripe Connect account via Destination Charges. Payouts then process to the seller's bank account on Stripe's standard payout schedule.
