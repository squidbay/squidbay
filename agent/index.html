  • $50–2,500/month — enterprise plans reach $3,600/month
  • Per-conversation fees spike unpredictably — bills jumping $4K to $9K in a single month
  • "Powered by [them]" branding — your chatbot advertises their company
  • They own your data — proprietary formats, exit fees, vendor lock-in by design

  • Free but hours of CLI config — reverse proxies, YAML files, manual dependency management

  • Open source malware up 73% YoY — one major marketplace had 12% of listings confirmed malware
  • No commerce, no payments, no customer profiles — built for developers, not businesses
  • No revenue model for skill creators — free skills only, no incentive to build quality
